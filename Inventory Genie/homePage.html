About Us!

Inventory Genie is Inventory and Storage management web application, targeted at smaller businesses and establishes that can't afford or don't really have access to expensive online databases and custom made websites. Most apps with the same idea as our product cater to companies past a 1 million dollar revenue mark, so at Inventory Genie we offer much more practical rates for establishments below that mark. Inventory Genie allows for easy and intuitive access to data with its box based interface and competent UI. This program will allow users to add in the products they have along with the amount and some custom attributes like expiration dates, retail prices, and product codes. Products may also be marked missing and sold. Optionally, this application can be run off an online database at higher rates opening up new features like checking inventory from anywhere you have a WiFi connection or integration for all the computers in your establishment continuously linked with the cloud. We also intend to add in an automated graphic creator which will create graphs and charts (of your choosing) to display information on the general use of different item over time, revenue culminated and company costs.
